I Was There

A Love Story Amid Dictatorship

Synopsis

In this love story set against the backdrop of dictatorship, Lidia waits for her boyfriend, Conrado Galdame, who never returned. What happened to him? Where is he? Through the testimonies of survivors of the Infierno Feced (Feced’s Hell), this documentary tells the events surrounding December 16, 1978, in Rosario, Argentina.
