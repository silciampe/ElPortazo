Voyeur

Voyeur: What hides behind each door of a home, what masks fall off when crossing the threshold of a home, what mirrors reflect the naked realities inside each house. And behind the others..... Miniseries that shows in consecutive episodes, the stories inside a three-story building and two apartments. Six stories undress inside the homes, while sharing common spaces in a building. An elderly couple, two lovers and their lovers, an apparently normal family, a famous man alone and a department of young students, which will reflect lives in parallel and in society, while we become little onlookers of daily events, spying, analyzing, criticizing, being a voyeur.
