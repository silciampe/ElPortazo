100 Bill

There are bubbles within bubbles, worlds and underworlds within others. Existential problems, butterfly effects, parallel worlds. Beings that depend on others and in turn these influence the following. Stories of the past and to come. At this precise moment, in order for you and us to be here, many generations and lineages had to pass within families. Skin color, sex, religion, power, love and affairs, kings and princesses... Everything, from the beginning to the end, is linked by the money, cash, bills, greens... Simple stories and of the others, linked to each other, through the circulation of a bill. What stories does money contain and bring with it, that when it passes from one hand to another, it buys love, pays for wounds, escapes death, gives joy or seizes sadness. Unitary miniseries, which proposes to discover all the stories that are saved, as a 100 bill circulates.
